How to Use Bodyssentials Body Oil

  1. Apply a small amount of oil onto your palms.
  2. Gently massage the oil into your skin in circular motions.
  3. Focus on dry areas like elbows and knees for added hydration.
  4. For best results, apply after a shower or bath while your skin is slightly damp.
